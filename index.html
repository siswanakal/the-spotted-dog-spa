Frequently Asked Questions

Do you require proof of vaccinations? ▼

Yes. For the safety of all pets, we require proof of Rabies, Distemper, and Bordetella vaccinations for all grooming appointments.

How long does a full groom take? ▼

Typically between 2 to 4 hours depending on the size and coat condition. We will text you 15 minutes before your pup is ready for pickup.

Can I stay with my pet during grooming? ▼

To keep pets calm and focused, we ask that you drop them off. Most pets behave much better when their owners aren't in their direct line of sight!
